About the M Division

The BMW M GmbH (formerly BMW Motorsport GmbH) is a subsidiary of BMW AG that was founded in 1972 to facilitate BMW's racing program. Over time, it transitioned to producing high-performance versions of various BMW models. The M stands for Motorsport, signifying the division's commitment to precision engineering, superior handling, and powerful engines. Every vehicle bearing the M badge is rigorously tested on the world's most demanding circuits, including the Nürburgring Nordschleife, to ensure it delivers a truly exhilarating and competitive driving experience.
