BMW M2 Coupe (G87)

₹1.03 Crore*

The smallest and most ferocious M car, the M2 is built for the purist driving experience. Its compact size and powerful performance make it an absolute thrill on the road and track. It features a stunning, aggressive design and offers an optional manual gearbox for ultimate driver engagement.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: S58 TwinPower Turbo Inline 6-Cylinder
  • Horsepower: 460 HP
  • 0-100 km/h: Approx. 4.1s (Manual) / 3.9s (Automatic)
  • Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)
  • Transmission: 6-Speed Manual or 8-Speed M Steptronic
  • Body Style: Compact Coupe
